About Us

We begin with three core premises:

  • Learning is hard
  • Learning alone is really hard
  • Some skills are expected of every programmer, but are not taught in school

hackerschool teaches basic, supplementary skills that you're expected to know outside school. The goal? To make learning these skills easier, by getting people past the initial hurdle, through workshops and classes.

Our Goal

NUS Hackers has conducted workshops in the past. What makes this different is its scale.

Though we are starting small, with only a few courses, we have a big ambition. The hope is to ultimately have a comprehensive list of workshops that incoming freshman and all NUS students can look forward to and take part in.

These courses will be continuously reviewed and conducted on a regular (yearly) basis.

Our Workshops

We charge a nominal fee for our workshops. This, in our experience, translates into a significant difference in terms of the crowd we attract as well as the general atmosphere during the workshops.

The team

We are NUS Hackers. To learn more about us, visit our site at http://nushackers.org.
