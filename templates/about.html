About Us

We begin with two core premises:

  • Learning is hard
  • Learning alone is really hard

hackerschool teaches basic, supplementary skills that are good to know, and not always covered in school. The goal? To make learning these skills easier by getting people past the initial hurdle through workshops and classes.

Our Goal

NUS Hackers has conducted workshops in the past. What makes this different is its scale.

Though we are starting small with only a few courses, we have a big ambition. The hope is to ultimately have a comprehensive list of workshops that all students can look forward to and take part in.

The Team

We are NUS Hackers. To learn more about us, visit our site at http://nushackers.org.
