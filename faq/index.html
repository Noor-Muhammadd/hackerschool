  • Should I bring my laptop?

    Definitely!

    All our workshops involve some hands-on portions, and you'll get much more out of them if you can follow along. We'll have facilitators around to help you out if you get stuck, so don't worry!

  • Can I register on the day of the workshop itself?

    Yes, you can!

    Do note, however, that available seats are handed out on a first-come-first-served basis, so there might not be any left by the time we start. Try to be early!

    TL;DR:a qualified yes.

  • I can't make it for the workshop I'm interested in, will there be another one on the topic this semester?

    No, we only conduct one workshop per topic in a semester.

    However, we'll usually post screencasts and/or materials from the workshops online at here.

  • I signed up for the workshop, but realize that I can't attend. Can I cancel?

    Yes! Just use the "contact us" link at the top of the page to drop us an email.
